  3. In order to determine if there are any locations outside of the US, you could take the data and filter based on longitude and latitude combinations that are inside of the US. You could filter based on state abbreviations that are from the US, however it is possible that a city abbreviation from another country could be the same as a state abbreviation from the US. However it is unlikely, and it may be easier to just use the city abbreviation.
